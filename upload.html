Submission Guidelines

Accepted Formats

  • Images: JPG, PNG, GIF (max 5MB each)
  • Videos: MP4, MOV (max 50MB)
  • Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX (max 10MB)
  • Presentations: PPT, PPTX (max 20MB)

Content Requirements

  • Include your name and age
  • Mention which challenge you completed
  • Describe your process and what you learned
  • Share any challenges you faced and how you overcame them

Privacy & Safety

  • Do not include personal information like address or phone number
  • Get parent/guardian permission if you're under 13
  • Only upload content you created yourself
  • Be respectful and positive in your descriptions
