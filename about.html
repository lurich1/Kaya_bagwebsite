About Kaaya

Our Story

Kaaya Mission Backpack was founded in 2020 by a group of educators and parents who wanted to create a fun, engaging way for children to learn important skills outside the traditional classroom.

The idea for Kaaya came from observing how children naturally learn through play and exploration. We wanted to harness that natural curiosity and channel it into meaningful learning experiences that develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

What started as a small collection of hands-on activities has grown into a comprehensive platform that reaches thousands of young learners around the world. Our mission remains the same: to inspire the next generation of thinkers, creators, and innovators.
